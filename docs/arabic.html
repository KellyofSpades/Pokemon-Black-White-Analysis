Arabic Translation Analysis

In the German-to-Arabic translation there are 115,701 total words and 16,615 unique word forms. In the English-to-Arabic translation there are 108,024 total words and 15,488 unique word forms. This means there is a 16,615 / 115,701 = 14.36% of the total words are unique in the German-to-Arabic translation. In the English translation: 15,488 / 108,024 = 14.33%. The texts are pretty similar in terms of richness of vocabulary.

On using Voyant, we found in the German-to-Arabic translation that words like I, do, the pokemon, and an Arabic word that has no meaning but used before calling a person’s name are used frequently. However, in the English-to-Arabic translation, these words appear frequently: do, already/has (a word that can either mean already or has), I, and the pokemon.
